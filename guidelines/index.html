Exus Services Freelancer Guidelines

Last Updated: August 7, 2019

    As a freelancer you will:

  • Be as professional as possible during client and guest interactions.
  • Have an online portfolio available to the public through the Discord Bot.
  • Not have more than 3 (three) active tickets at a time.
  • Try your hardest to finish every commission on the discussed timeframe.
  • Make sure that all of your work is the best possible quality for your abilities.
  • Not claim a commission that you don't have time to finish or are unable to do so.
  • Claim atleast 1 commission a month.
  • Do everything you can to make the client satisfied.
  • Make sure to reserve some time for discussing the details after you claim a commission. Do not keep the client waiting.
  • Try to give the clients frequent updates on the work you're doing.
  • Never share your client's private information or data with anyone.
  • Try not to talk with the client about a commission outside of the ticket.
  • Not leave the discord server while you have active/unfinished commissions.
  • Not leave the discord server or resign from the team without informing Management first.
  • Listen to any and all advice or requests from the Management. Please report any abuse of this rule to the CEO (ejer#9484).
  • Report any and all bug and loopholes in our systems to Management straight away. Do not exploit or abuse any of them.
  • Not talk about any confidential information from private chats that are not available to the public unless it is allowed by Management.

Commission process

1. You can claim a commission by reacting to a commission message in #commissions with the âœ… (yes) emoji.

2. Once you claim the commission, head over to the ticket channel you just got added to. All tickets appear at the top of the discord server.

3. Next, discuss the commission details and the price with the client.

4. If you are not able to finish or make the requested product please tag @Management. In that case you will be removed from the ticket and the commission will be reposted. If you are able to fulfill the reqest the ticket will be marked as Active by management.

5. Before you start work, the client has to pay the first 50% of the full price. You can either tag @Management or execute the command -pay <50% of the price> In most cases management will execute it for you and after we confirm the payment and give you the green light the ticket will be marked as Half by management.

6. You can now start work on the project, make sure to give the client frequent updates on your progress. Make sure to watermark any previews.

7. If the client does not like the previews of the product you made, he may request a refund. In that case please tag @Management and we will handle the situation from there. If the client does like the final product you can either tag @Management or execute the command -pay <50% of the price> and after we confirm the payment and give you the green light the ticket will be marked as Paid by management and you will send the final unwatermarked product to the client.

8. After the client recieves your product the ticket will be marked as Completed. You are allowed to ask for a vouch on any platform that you and the client might share.

9. After the ticket is marked as Completed and we confirm we got all of the money, you will be paid your cut as soon as possible.
